silver

The Moon Silver motorized beds come in different configurations, primarily varying in size, color, and material. The first variant features a 90x200 cm size with 15 cm cone legs in smoked ash, complemented by a Primo 84 light grey upholstery. The second option is a slightly smaller 80x200 cm size, retaining the same pocket 5-zone mattress and a Primo 84 light grey finish. The third variant also maintains the 90x200 cm size but differs in its upholstery, which is in Primo 84 light grey, providing a different aesthetic while still offering the comfort of the pocket 5-zone mattress.

Overall, while all three variants share the same pocket 5-zone feature and motorized functionality, they differ in dimensions—80x200 cm versus 90x200 cm—and in upholstery options, allowing customers to choose based on their size preferences and aesthetic tastes.
