Moon Headboards Overview

Discover the elegant and customizable Moon headboard collection, designed to enhance your bedroom aesthetic while providing comfort and style.

  1. Moon Bronze Headboards: The Moon Bronze headboards are available in sizes ranging from 120cm to 200cm, catering to various bed dimensions. Choose from three fabric options: Primo 05 in beige, Primo 84 in light grey, and Primo 96 in anthracite, allowing for personalized style and comfort.

  2. Moon Gold Headboards: The Moon Gold headboard collection features a variety of sizes from 120cm to 200cm, ensuring a perfect fit for your bed. With three fabric choices—Primo 05 beige, Primo 84 light grey, and Primo 96 anthracite—these headboards offer a blend of luxury and versatility to match your bedroom decor.

  3. Moon Silver Headboards: The Moon Silver headboards come in multiple sizes, including 120cm, 140cm, 160cm, 180cm, and 200cm, providing flexibility for different room layouts. Available in three upholstery options—Primo 05 beige, Primo 84 light grey, and Primo 96 anthracite—these headboards combine style with functional design.

Elevate your bedroom with the Moon headboard collection, where quality meets customization.
