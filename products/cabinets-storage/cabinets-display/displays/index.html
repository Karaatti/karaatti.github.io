Mystery Shelves
The Mystery shelf line features two size variants: the 150 cm shelf and the 120 cm shelf, both showcasing a sleek black finish. The 150 cm variant is perfect for larger storage needs, making it an ideal choice for extensive collections or as a statement piece in spacious areas. The 120 cm version caters to smaller spaces, offering a compact yet stylish storage solution without compromising on design.
